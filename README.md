The goal of this project was to analyze historical stock data for several major tech companies (AAPL, GOOG, MSFT, NFLX) and visualize key metrics in a clear, concise, and interactive dashboard.

Key Metrics & Insights Visualized:
Market Adjusted Close Price: Tracking monthly price movements.

Average Daily Return: Understanding the volatility and typical performance.

Maximum Open Price by Ticker: Identifying peak daily opening values.

Sum of Daily Return by Ticker: Highlighting overall proportional returns contribution.

Monthly Volume of Company: Analyzing trading liquidity and investor interest.

Monthly Price Range: Showing the spread between high and low prices for the month.

Scatter Plot of Daily Return vs. Volume: Exploring the relationship between trading activity and returns.
